FAQ

How can I get money by renting my stuff?
You simply login our service from any device, post a picture, video and a description of whatever you want to share and fix a daily fee. People from our community will start to book your goods, they will come to pick it up and they will bring it back at the end of the period they pay for. Our ranking system will ensure lowest price to most reliable users, and a special insurance will allow you to sleep sweet dreams, knowing you did the right thing by let other people enjoy the good that would have been a waste otherwise
What kind of stuff I can share on Concrete Share?
Basically anything that can be easily moved, music instrument and DIY tools are the perfect objects. Motorbikes and bycicles, like cars and why not, you own house. Other people can enjoy your precious video camera while you finally clean the garder with that professional cuttter. If you need to organize a grill party or to build a wall, the only limit to what you can share is your fantasy. Statistically, the most requested objects are expensive professional tools, often used rarely but providing a big advantage.
